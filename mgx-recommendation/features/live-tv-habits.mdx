---

title="Live TV Habits"
description="Display the user's favorite shows on live TV."

---

Live TV recommendations are a personalized EPG with an immediate access to the user's favorites programs.

The feature analyzes each user’s TV habits and is connected to the TV programme in order to display a personalized line of on air recommendations.

Live TV recommendations identify the next occurrences of the TV shows watched on a regular basis by the user.


### Endpoint

```
GET /users/{user}/linearTvHabits
```

More details on the [API documentation](/dev/null)


**Did you know?**

You can mix this feature with a fallback business rule, in order to show most popular content in case the user doesn’t have enough favorite shows.
