---

title="Surprise"
description="Display explainable lines by randomly combining two keywords."

---

Automatically generate explainable thematic lists created by combining two keywords. Each pair of keywords displays a list of titles corresponding to the topic combination.

E.G. one line could randomly be “Love stories and World War I”, displaying titles such as *Legends of the Fall, The African Queen, Testament of Youth*.

Another one could be “Disney animations and wildlife”, with *The Lion King, The Jungle Book, Tarzan, ..*

### Endpoint

```
GET /surprise/pairs/{universe}
```

More details on the [API documentation](/dev/null)

### Use case

Implement a surprise line on the homepage that resets every 24h to give users daily new ideas of content to watch.
